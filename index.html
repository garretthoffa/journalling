Online Journal

Access Your Journal Anytime, Anywhere

This website saves you journal online, and allows you to access it or download it from anywhere.

Storage

We store your journal for free on our database so you dont have to worry about losing it, we also allow you to download your journals.

How It Works

You can login and create an account from the login tab. After you have logged in you will be able to see all of your entries, and also have to option to create a new entry. Clicking on an entry will bring up a page so you can view or click the button to edit the entry or save it to your computer.
